Target-Audience

Description

This site is designed for people who want to grow their small or medium business, either by advertising their products, finding new partners, or growing their business network. It is also thought for people who want to look for local businesses and tourists.

Personas

Persona 1

30-year-old man, married, self-employed, with his own business selling flower arrangements for any occasion, graduated from university with a degree in business administration. He has three children. He has had moderate success with his business but hopes to grow it by expanding his business network to find better suppliers and gain publicity.

Persona 2

A 26-year-old woman who is planning a trip with her boyfriend to that city, they do not have children, she is a graduate student of a degree in dentistry. She wants to know what businesses there are in the area to buy food, souvenirs, clothes, accessories, she would like to be able to have addresses and telephone numbers at hand to schedule appointments and that her experience on the trip be pleasant at all times. She also hopes to get to know the city well because maybe she and her boyfriend will move after they get married and she wants to see if she can have a good future in the market of that city.

Scenarios

  • Is the site easy to find application requirements?
  • What kind of services does the chamber commerce provide?
  • I want to check up coming activities.
  • I want to grow my business with more publicity.
  • Does the site have clear content and design?
  • Can I easily find a place to have dinner with my family?
